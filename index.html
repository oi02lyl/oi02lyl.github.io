About me

I am a senior research scientist at the Megagon Labs. Before joining the Megagon Labs, I was a PhD graduate from UC San Diego (UCSD). My advisors are Alin Deutsch and Victor Vianu. I have worked on a wide variety of research topics. My recent research interests include data management and natural language processing particularly in applying ML techniques to data integration and mining tasks. My PhD thesis is on the Verification of Data-driven workflows, a research direction that lies in the intersection of Database Theory, Software Model Checking, and Business Process Management. Before joining UCSD, I obtained my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. During my undergraduate, I was also interested in Data Mining.

I was an intern in Google Research during 2013 Summer and in Microsoft Research during 2014 Summer. I was an intern at IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Center during 2017 Summer.
