About me

I am a 5th year Computer Science PhD stduent in UC San Diego (UCSD). My advisors are Alin Deutsch and Victor Vianu. I am currently working on Verification of Data-driven workflows, which is a research direction that lies in the intersection of Database Theory and Software Model Checking, with applications in Business Process Management etc. Before joining UCSD, I obtained my undergradate degree in Computer Science from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. During my undergraduate, I was also interested in Data Mining.

I was an intern in Google Research during 2013 Summer and in Microsoft Research during 2014 Summer.
